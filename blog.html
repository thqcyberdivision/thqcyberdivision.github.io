10-Step Cybersecurity Checklist for Small Businesses

Posted by THQ Cyber Division Team on September 26th, 2025 | Cybersecurity

Is your business protected from the latest threats? Follow our actionable 10-step checklist to secure your email, devices, cloud accounts, and more. Download a printable version for your office!

10 Steps to Protect Your Small Business

  1. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  3. Regularly update all software and operating systems.
  4. Train employees to recognize phishing attempts.
  5. Back up your data regularly to secure, offsite locations.
  6. Install and maintain antivirus software.
  7. Restrict admin rights to essential personnel only.
  8. Secure your Wi-Fi networks with strong encryption.
  9. Monitor account activity for suspicious behavior.
  10. Have an incident response plan (know who to call and what to do).

5 IT Support Tips Every Remote Worker Should Know

Posted by Support Specialist on September 26th, 2025 | IT Support

Remote work is here to stay. Learn five essential IT support practices—from device updates to secure backups—to keep your workflow smooth and your data safe.

Essential Tips

  1. Always keep your operating system and apps up to date.
  2. Use a VPN when connecting to public or home Wi-Fi.
  3. Back up important files to secure cloud or external storage.
  4. Install endpoint security and enable firewalls.
  5. Contact IT support early if you notice strange behavior or performance issues.
