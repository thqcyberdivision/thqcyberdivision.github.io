Our Mission

THQ Cyber Division protects society from cyber threats by helping organizations, researchers, and security teams safeguard digital assets, reputation, and operations. We deliver trusted cyber threat intelligence, resilient infrastructure services, and enterprise-grade certificates — all guided by privacy, ethics, and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To create a safer digital world by empowering organizations and individuals to proactively defend against cyber threats, fostering trust, resilience, and responsible use of technology across society.

Our Services

  • Cyber Threat Intelligence – Actionable feeds, prioritized alerts, and analyst-led briefings.
  • Secure Hosting – Hardened dedicated hosts, isolated instances, and secure network segmentation.
  • Sectigo SSL/TLS Certificates – EV, OV, wildcard, and multi-domain certificates with full lifecycle support.
  • Sophos Security Solutions – Endpoint detection & response, managed detection (MDR), and firewall integration.
  • Custom Security Software – Tools and integrations for research teams, licensing available on request.

Core Values

  • Client privacy and responsible handling of sensitive information

  • Responsive and practical technical support
